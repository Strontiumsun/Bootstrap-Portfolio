About Me

Woomy! This is Izzy here! I gotta say, I think the easiest way to connect with people is by sharing the things you enjoy! When two people like the same thing, they have lots to talk about. So here are some things I love!

I love stories! I of course write my own story and publish it online in the form of an online comic or “webcomic.” It’s called Heroes of Thantopolis and it is totally free to read! I’ve been working on the comic for almost 5 years now so there is a lot of content to peruse. I love to read books and get immersed in stories. I love hearing about my friends’ stories too!

I love YouTube! YouTube videos are so entertaining and there are so many to look through. The categories of videos I watch the most are Gaming and Animation. When it comes to gaming, I love Nintendo games and watch a lot of Nintendo channels. My two favorite games to watch on YouTube are Splatoon 2 (which I am great at) and Smash (which I wish I was great at).

I love nature! Animals, plants, rocks, bodies of water, you name it, I love it! I live right near the beach and in the summer I love to go and watch the waves on the shore. I love visiting the Chicago Botanical Gardens. I know its winter now, but they have a lovely greenhouse exhibit that is open all year round! And when it comes to animals, I pretty much love all of them. Now I don’t have any pets, but I DO have a ton of animal plushies, so that fills the void a bit.

And most of all, I love to learn! Whether it’s learning from a book, from a newspaper, from the TV, from a magazine, from online, from classes, from the people in my life, I love to learn! I have an unquenchable fire in me that longs to consume more and more information. I’m so excited to learn together with everyone!

Thanks for reading!
